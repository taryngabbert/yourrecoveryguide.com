Jerry Gabbert is an experienced recovery professional who specializes in transitional recovery coaching.

Transitions

Transitioning back home from a residential treatment facility is statistically proven to be one of the most challenging times for recovering addicts, and the return to familiar territory can often result in a return to familiar habits without 24-hour assistance and support.

One on One

Jerry works with his clients individually to ensure they’re able to successfully re-immerse themselves in daily life. Each transitional recovery program is customized to fit the needs of his individual clients and cater to their unique circumstances.

Personalized

Together, you and Jerry can set the personal goals you want to achieve during early recovery. He will supply the structure, accountability, and resources necessary for you to obtain them.

Jerry Gabbert

Certified Recovery Coach

Jerry Gabbert struggled with multiple addictions from a young age, and over the course of thirty years he spent time in twenty residential treatment facilities, including some of the finest and most successful centers in the world.

Like many addicts, Jerry struggled each time he left treatment because he was unable to implement the strategies he’d learned there once outside the walls of the facility. After decades of unsuccessful attempts at recovery, however, Jerry discovered that the support he needed was a coach who could provide guidance and accountability during the weeks following his transition back home.

Finally, with this missing link in place, he was able to maintain his sobriety. Now, Jerry has dedicated his life to helping others find their way. He understands that finding a balance within recovery work, and finding fun ways to enjoy recovery, are crucial parts of the transition process. Jerry has worked with professional and celebrity clients all over the world to share the recovery strategies he’s learned and ensure their continued success even while traveling abroad and meeting the rigorous demands of their work schedules.

He maintains contact with the nation’s top treatment professionals and finest treatment facilities. If you’re struggling with the transition back home and need help, call Jerry now. Let him put his years of experience along with the world’s top resources to work for you.
